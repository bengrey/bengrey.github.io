Our dream and vision

is to see how God transforms the peoples of Eastern Europe!

The first Eastern European Leadership Forum (EELF) took place in 2011. It is a subsidiary of the European Leadership Forum, which has a strong influence and tradition in Western European Christianity through uniting, equipping and resourcing Evangelical leaders to restore the Biblical Church and the evangelization of Europe. Since its foundation, the Forum has become an annual event that includes projects throughout the year. Today, about 600 leaders from Eastern Europe are part of the EELF. The Eastern European Leadership Forum is a platform for evangelical leaders from the countries of the former Soviet Union, where they unite together for the sake of fulfillment of the Great Commandment of Christ.

The EELF is organized by the Eastern European Reformation ministry. The dream and vision of our ministry is to see how God transforms the peoples of Eastern Europe through the Biblical Church. We want to see a renewed Church, which persistently and innovatively spreads the gospel to the Eastern European peoples, the Church that is faithful to Scripture, testifies of Christ through its holy life, and gives God's answers to the challenges of the modern world.

Our goal

is to help the church develop its effectiveness in society. The Forum aims to support evangelical leaders through three key aspects:

Unity

Building bridges between different evangelical environments and ministries for further collaboration. Unity makes it possible to reach a level that cannot be achieved by the separate leaders on their own, no matter how good their ministry is.

Mentoring

Experienced leaders share their experiences during lectures, round tables, sermons and talk shows, as well as in private informal conversations with the participants. Each year, more than 80 speakers from around the world are present at the Forum.

Resource

Promotion of programs aimed at the evangelization of the Eastern European countries and their cultures.

OUR VALUES

is our guide

The activity of the ministry of the Eastern European Reformation, and hence of the East European Leadership Forum, is based on a clear world-view paradigm, which we outlined in "10 Points of Biblical Worldview in the Public Square":

1 - THEOLOGY

- We stand for the classical Biblical theology which is faithful to the Evangelical tradition approved through the ages and experienced by the mankind.

- We are against liberal theology in its modernist or postmodern form that led the Church in Western Europe to the deep crisis.

REFORMATION IN UKRAINE

and our projects

Since 2016, the Eastern European Reformation ministry has been involved in a number of key projects of the Year of the Reformation (R500). In particular, we contributed some key ideas and materials as far as some of them have already been developed in Belarus for the 450th anniversary of the Reformation in this country.

interactive report on our activities and projects in 2016/17

Since 2018, we have been actively involved in the Reformation Movement as a continuation of the R500 committees. Our ministry supports the functioning of the Reformation of Christian Leadership department, whose activities are primarily aimed at disseminating the necessary ideological knowledge among those Christian leaders who will be able to provide the socially active members of their churches with the necessary pastoral care. In this direction, we began to distribute educational materials, as well as develop the project "The School of Reformation" as a training platform for leaders.
